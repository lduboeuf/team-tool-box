Welcome to Team Tool Box, a project to help you build teams or find members randomly!

This tool may be usefull for trainers, teachers, managers and everyone who work with or within groups.

It may respond to whenever we need to pick someone, don't find volonteers, define teams (practical exercices, collective thinking, etc...) or need a passing order.

This site does not hold any of your datas. Thanks to remoteStorage, you can keep and synchronize your datas accross devices. Just click on the top-right icon, you can host your own remoteStorage server or choose between existing providers.

Developper: This is a unhosted Single Page Application built with several tiny components, more information and source code here: https://github.com/lduboeuf/team-tool-box

Also, demonstrate that for little apps, there is no need to use big MV* Frameworks and program frameworks' oriented code.
